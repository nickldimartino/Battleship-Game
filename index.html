GAME RULES:
GOAL: Be the first to sink all five of your opponent's ships.
GAMEPLAY: Pick a fleet layout by placing the five ships on your board vertically or horizontally. Boat positions cannot be changed once the game has begun. Take turns alternating guesses to hit each other's ships.
Legend:
-Red squares designate hits.
-White squares designate misses.
-Grey squares designate your ships.
