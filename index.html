GAME RULES:
GOAL: Be the first to sink all five of your opponent's ships.
GAMEPLAY: Take turns alternating guesses to hit each other's ships. The first peson to sink all of their opponent's ships wins.
LEGEND:
-Red squares designate hits.
-White squares designate misses.
-Grey squares designate your ships.
-Lightgrey squares designate unsaved boat position.
BOAT PLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS:
GOAL: Place all fice boats on your game board.
HOW TO: Boats must be placed horiozontally or vertically and they can't be changed once the game has started. Pick a ship and place that number of squares in a row. Then, click any one of that ships' squares to lock it. Repeat for each ship.
LEGEND:
-Aircraft Carrier: 5 squares
-Battleship: 4 squares
-Destroyer: 3 squares
-Submarine: 3 squares
-Patrol Boat: 2 squares
